Huaijin Ran

Trustworthy software intelligence for compliant, secure and accountable systems.

I am a student researcher at Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University. My work studies how large language models and program-analysis techniques can make software systems more compliant, secure and accountable. I also collaborate on spatiotemporal and multimodal learning.

Current focus

Research

I work at the intersection of software engineering, artificial intelligence and security.

01

Software compliance & privacy

Benchmarks and methods for detecting GDPR and policy violations in real-world Android software.

02

LLMs for software engineering

Evaluation and design of language-model agents for analysis, repair and evidence-driven engineering workflows.

03

Program analysis & trustworthy AI

Static reasoning, specification inference, provenance and accountability for AI-assisted software.
